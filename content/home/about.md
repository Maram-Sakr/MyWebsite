Maram Sakr is a joint Ph.D. student at the Mechanical Engineering Department at the University of British Columbia, Canada and the Department of Electrical and Computer Systems Engineering at Monash University. Her doctorate research is about effective teaching for robot learning from demonstration. Maram's research vision is to allow everyday users to intuitively teach robots by demonstrations and provide high-quality demonstrations for the robot to learn from.  She is co-supervised by Prof. Elizabeth Croft, Prof. Dana Kulic and Dr. H.F. Machiel Van der Loos.
